Upcoming Event

Global Fusion Night

January 24 - 26, 2024

Royal Mandaya Hotel, Palma Gil St., Davao City, Philippines

Registration

Join us for an unforgettable evening of culinary exploration! "Global Fusion Night" is a celebration of diverse flavors, bringing together the best of international cuisines right to your kitchen. Mark your calendars for a night of taste bud tantalization and cultural immersion.

Event Highlights:

  1. Live Cooking Demos: Renowned chefs will take center stage to demonstrate the art of creating signature dishes from around the world. Get ready to pick up insider tips and tricks that will elevate your home cooking game.
  2. Tasting Stations: Embark on a flavor-filled journey with tasting stations featuring dishes from Asia, Europe, the Americas, and beyond. Sample a smorgasbord of global delights prepared by our expert culinary team.
  3. Interactive Workshops: Dive deep into the intricacies of specific cuisines with our interactive workshops. From mastering the art of sushi rolling to perfecting the balance of spices in Indian cuisine, there's something for every aspiring chef.
  4. Q&A Sessions: Have burning culinary questions? Our panel of chefs and food enthusiasts will be ready to answer all your queries. Whether you're a kitchen rookie or a seasoned pro, there's always something new to learn.

How to Participate:

  1. RSVP: Secure your spot by registering for the event. Limited slots available, so don't miss out on this culinary adventure.
  2. Spread the Word: Share the excitement with your friends and family. The more, the merrier!
  3. Prepare Your Kitchen: Get ready to cook along with our live demos or simply sit back, relax, and enjoy the show.
Don't miss this opportunity to broaden your culinary horizons and connect with food enthusiasts from around the globe. "Global Fusion Night" promises to be a feast for the senses, and we can't wait to share this culinary journey with you! Stay tuned for more updates on upcoming events and culinary experiences. Cheers to a world of flavors!
